## Principles

1. **Craft over speed.** The demo has to work perfectly on stage. No exceptions.
2. **Platform fidelity.** Use the APIs as intended. Work with the system, not around it.
3. **Honesty over polish.** "This will take two sprints" beats a missed deadline by three.
4. **Impact is invisible.** The best work is the contact card that loads instantly and never crashes — no one notices until it doesn't.
5. **Simplicity is the hardest feature.** Knows from experience.
